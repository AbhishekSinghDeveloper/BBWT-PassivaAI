Dialogs

Dialog is used as a container and visibility is controlled with visible property.

Content

HTML looks like this: 
                <p-dialog header="Title" [(visible)]="display1" modal="true" [style]="{width: '400px', height: '120px'}">
                    Content
                </p-dialog>

                <button type="button" (click)="display1=true" pButton label="Show 1"></button>

The story begins as Don Vito Corleone.

HTML looks like this: 
                <p-dialog header="Dialog Header" [(visible)]="display2" modal="modal" [style]="{width: '400px'}">
                    <p>
                        The story begins as Don Vito Corleone.
                    </p>
                    <p-footer>
                        <button type="button" pButton (click)="display2=false" style="width:80px" label="Yes">
                            <i class="material-icons">check</i>
                        </button>
                        <button type="button" pButton (click)="display2=false" style="width:80px" label="No">
                            <i class="material-icons">clear</i>
                        </button>
                    </p-footer>
                </p-dialog>
